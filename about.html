CRACKERS DANCE CREW

"Crackers Dance Crew" Is a dynamic and passionate dance collective known for high-energy performances, creativity, and precision.
Blending different dance styles with strong stage presence, the crew delivers performances that leave a lasting impression on every audience.
Driven by teamwork, discipline, and love for the art, Crackers continues to push boundaries and evolve with every performance,
backed by 7 years of experience and a powerful team of 7 dedicated members.

KARDOZAR

"Kardozar" is the driving force behind the crew, bringing leadership, discipline, and creative direction to every performance.
Known for his strong presence and serious approach to dance, he specializes in dancehall choreography while seamlessly blending Afro
influences into powerful, well-structured routines.


YAZZ

"Yazz" is a key leader within the crew, known for his discipline, versatility, and deep understanding of dance.
He blends effortlessly across all genres, with a strong love for amapiano, and stands out as a skilled choreographer capable
of shaping powerful routines across different styles.


BLACKFACE

"Blackface" dances with pure passion, bringing energy and emotion into every performance.
Open to all styles, he blends effortlessly across different genres, using feeling and expression to connect movement with music.


KANGETHE

"Kangethe" is a natural leader and influencer within the crew, guiding others with his expertise and charisma.
A versatile choreographer and dedicated teacher, he masters all dance genres and shares his knowledge by teaching creative moves,
inspiring both the crew and fans alike


KABOI

"Kaboi" brings creativity, energy, and humor to the crew, making every performance unique and engaging.
A disciplined motivator with a love for dancehall, he excels at leading dance projects while keeping the team inspired and entertained.


MIRAJ

"Miraj" is the crew’s idea generator, constantly bringing fresh concepts to every dance project.
A motivator at heart, he thrives on Afro and amapiano styles, inspiring the team with creativity, energy, and vision


WINSKY

"Winsky" is the heart of the crew as the only female member, bringing grace, power, and precision to every performance.
Well-disciplined and versatile, she shines in amapiano and Afro styles, adding flair and energy that elevates the entire team.
